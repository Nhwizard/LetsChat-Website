About LetsChat

LetsChat is a conversational companion app. It is designed to provide friendly conversation, text chat, and call-style interaction for users who want a simple place to talk.

The app may include free, Pro, and Premium access options. Some access may be provided through Apple App Store subscriptions, and some access may be provided through facility access codes.

Important Safety Note

LetsChat is for friendly conversation and companionship. It is not a medical, emergency, legal, financial, or mental-health service. If you feel unsafe, unwell, or need urgent help, contact facility staff, family, emergency services, or a qualified professional.
