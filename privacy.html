This Privacy Policy explains how LetsChat AI LLC (“LetsChat,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you use the LetsChat mobile app, website, and related services.

1. Information Stored on Your Device

LetsChat stores certain information locally on your device so the app can function, remember your settings, and provide a more personalized experience. This may include:

  • Chat history and recent conversation context;
  • Your preferred name, interests, and conversation style, if you choose to enter them;
  • Text-length, voice, caption, display, and Call Mode settings;
  • Your selected on-device conversation model;
  • Local message-usage counters;
  • Local subscription-entitlement status received from Apple; and
  • Other app preferences needed to provide the service.

This information remains on your device unless you intentionally provide it to us through a support request or another feature described in this Policy.

2. Artificial Intelligence and On-Device Processing

LetsChat uses an on-device language model to generate conversational responses. The model runs locally on the user’s device. LetsChat does not send ordinary chat messages, conversation history, or personalization information to OpenAI or another third-party cloud generative-AI service for response generation.

The on-device language model may process:

  • Text entered by the user;
  • Recent conversation context;
  • Preferred name, interests, and conversation style, if voluntarily provided; and
  • Other information the user voluntarily includes in a conversation.

This information is processed locally to generate responses and personalize the conversation. LetsChat may also offer an optional larger conversation model. That model is downloaded to the user’s device and performs response generation locally after installation.

3. Model Downloads and Network Requests

If you choose to download an optional conversation model, the app contacts our file-hosting provider to retrieve the model file. That provider may receive technical network information that is normally transmitted when downloading a file, such as an IP address, request time, and basic request logs. Chat messages and conversation history are not included in the model download request.

4. Microphone and Speech Recognition

Call Mode uses microphone access so you can speak to the app. Apple’s Speech Recognition framework converts spoken audio into text.

Depending on the device, language, network connection, and operating-system configuration, speech recognition may be processed on the device or by Apple’s speech-recognition services. LetsChat does not send voice recordings or conversation audio to its own servers for AI response generation.

You can control microphone and speech-recognition permissions in iOS Settings. Apple’s own processing is governed by Apple’s privacy policies and terms.

5. App Store Purchases and Subscriptions

If you purchase a Pro or Premium subscription through the Apple App Store, Apple handles the payment transaction. LetsChat receives limited subscription-entitlement information through StoreKit so the app can determine which plan is active and restore eligible purchases.

LetsChat does not receive your complete payment-card number or Apple Account password. Payment and subscription information processed by Apple is governed by Apple’s terms and privacy policies.

6. Support Communications

If you contact us for support, we may receive your email address and any information you choose to include, such as screenshots, logs, device details, or conversation examples. We use this information to respond to your request, diagnose problems, improve reliability, and protect the service.

Please avoid sending highly sensitive personal information in support messages unless it is necessary for us to assist you.

7. How We Use Information

We use information only as reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain LetsChat;
  • Generate and personalize conversations locally on the device;
  • Recognize valid App Store subscription entitlements;
  • Respond to support requests;
  • Improve reliability, accessibility, and safety;
  • Detect, prevent, or investigate misuse and technical problems; and
  • Comply with legal obligations.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not use chat content for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

Information may be shared only:

  • With service providers that help us operate the website, deliver model files, or provide support;
  • With Apple when you use Apple services such as StoreKit or Speech Recognition;
  • When required by law or a valid legal process;
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users, LetsChat, or others; or
  • As part of a business transaction, subject to applicable law and appropriate safeguards.

9. Data Retention

Local app information remains on your device until you reset it, delete it through available app controls, or delete the app. Support communications may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to answer your request, maintain records, prevent abuse, resolve disputes, or comply with legal obligations.

10. Resetting or Deleting Local Data

You can delete locally stored app information by using the Reset Local User Data option inside LetsChat. Deleting the app from your device also removes locally stored app data from that device.

To ask about information that may be held by LetsChat through support communications, email LetsChatAILLC@gmail.com.

11. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures designed to protect information. However, no storage or transmission method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Children

LetsChat is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us so we can review the situation and take appropriate action.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised version on this page and update the “Last updated” date above. Material changes may also be communicated in the app or through another appropriate method.

14. Contact

Questions or privacy requests can be sent to LetsChatAILLC@gmail.com.
