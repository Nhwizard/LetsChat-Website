Pick Panic! Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 30, 2026

Pick Panic! is a party ranking and voting game operated by LetsChat Ai LLC. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how you can contact us.

Information We Collect

Pick Panic! is designed to work without user accounts. We do not ask you to create an account, provide a name, provide an email address, or submit personal profile information to play the game.

The app may allow players to type player names or custom list content. This information is used for gameplay and app functionality. It is intended to stay on your device unless you choose to share it through device features or future app functionality.

Purchases

Pick Panic! may offer non-consumable in-app purchases. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card number, billing address, or other payment credentials.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

Pick Panic! does not currently use third-party advertising, cross-app tracking, or third-party analytics SDKs. If this changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

Children's Privacy

Pick Panic! is intended as a general-audience party game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information with advertisers. Purchase handling is managed by Apple through the App Store.

Data Security

We use reasonable efforts to keep the app safe and reliable. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
